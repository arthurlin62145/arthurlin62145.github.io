Welcome to my personal website!

I am an incoming Empirical Research Fellow in the Finance Department at the Kellogg School of Management, Northwestern University.

My research interests lie in development economics, labor economics, and economic history. More broadly, I am interested in how culture, institutions, and historical events shape economic behavior and long-run development outcomes.

My current research examines the horizontal transmission of gender norms across ethnic groups, with a particular focus on how social interactions and exposure to different communities influence attitudes toward gender roles and female labor force participation.
